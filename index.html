About Me

"Expression is my goal in art. Without that, the humanity is lost"

Currently studying Global Business and Design (GBDA) at the University of Waterloo, a fusion of design, business, tech, and humanities that makes me a versatile creative thinker.


My artistic journey started with acrylic painting at 15, when I found a fascination for color, composition, and artistic storytelling. My art explores techniques into abstract design, and realism.


My painting skills are continually improving. I have found that, as I move forwards in my life, my taste has changed tremendously. Styles that have once interested me have become drab and uninspired. Check back on this website to see how I've grown and how my art continues to evolve.
